[ ABOUT ] Weihai Emlta Chemical

Built on technical depth, not just supply.

Since 2008, EMLTA has grown from a focused additives supplier into a full PVC partner — additives, machinery and the expertise to make them work together.

From the CEO
“We didn't set out to simply sell additives — we set out to make PVC processing cleaner, safer and more dependable.”
Leonard Zhao Founder & CEO, EMLTA Chem Co., Ltd.

Leonard Zhao has spent more than 20 years in the specialty chemicals industry. Before founding EMLTA, he held senior commercial roles at Rohm and Haas — one of the world's leading specialty chemicals companies — serving as Area Sales Manager and then Sales Manager for Northern China, building deep expertise in PVC additives and the processors who rely on them.

He founded Weihai EMLTA Chemical Industry in 2008 and has grown it into a trusted supplier across plasticizers, impact modifiers, processing aids, stabilizers and resins. Drawing on years on the commercial and technical front line, he built EMLTA's reputation on technical depth and consistency — working alongside customers on formulation, scale-up and quality assurance rather than simply shipping product.

[ HERITAGE ] A Rohm & Haas lineage

From a Rohm & Haas joint venture to EMLTA.

EMLTA's technical DNA runs through two decades of acrylic (ACR) PVC-additive expertise — from Jinhong Polymer, through the Rohm & Haas joint venture, to Jinhass today.

1999

Weihai Jinhong Polymer begins producing and selling acrylic (ACR) additives for PVC, earning a strong reputation in the market.

2007

Jinhong Polymer and Rohm & Haas (USA) form a joint venture — Weihai Jinhong Rohm & Haas Chemical — run as one of China's safest, most environment-friendly ACR plants.

2010

Renamed Weihai Jinhass Chemical after a shareholder change — carrying forward the venture's management system, technology and customer-first culture.

Today

EMLTA Chemical is the sole overseas agent for Jinhong Rohm & Haas — and a China market leader in chemical distribution.

Managed by Rohm & Haas, the venture built advanced manufacturing technology and excellent, consistent product quality — earning deep recognition from customers across PVC building and construction materials: window profile, siding, fence, pipe, fitting and foaming sheet.

Jinhass carries that legacy forward, holding environment, health and safety at the core of how it operates — and continuing to invest in R&D to develop creative, high-quality products for evolving market demands.
